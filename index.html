Welcome to World Wildlife

We share our planet with millions of species of plants and animals - a wondrous variety of wildlife that enriches our lives in so many ways.

Restoring Balance: Reviving Our Planet's Wildlife for a Thriving Future

This complex web of life provides the natural systems we depend on – giving us essentials like water, clean air, fertile soils, and a stable climate. It gives us food, medicines, and materials, and supports millions of jobs. It also inspires people around the world – making our lives richer in all sorts of ways. But our planet’s wildlife is in crisis – numbers have fallen by more than half since 1970, and species are going extinct at an alarming rate. We need to reverse this loss of nature and create a future where wildlife and people thrive again.
